SHPE iOS App

The new SHPE iOS App aims to provide seemless access to SHPE resources at UF and increase involvement. The app will allow students to access their points to quantify their attendance.

Cell Detector AI

To make data collection more efficient in Dr. Tang's Mechanobiology lab, the Cell Detector AI utilizes object detection from the YOLOv5 library by Ultralytics to detect the number of cells in images taken by our lab's microscope, calculate cell area, and track cell growth.
