GMan or Bootlegger

Description

July 2023

In Robosub 2022 and 2023, one of the tasks is to distinguish between two images. One of a Bootlegger and the other of a GMan. In order to successfully accomplish this task, the underwater autonomous vehicle (UAV) must use computer vision to distinguish between the two. Training the YOLOv5 object detection model by Ultralytics, I was able to achieve detections upwards of 90% confidence on live feeds.

Features

Constrained Sample Size

Working on this project remotely meant I did not have footage from the lab directly from our UAV. However, acknowledging that recordings of the obstacle course were available on the internet, I used screen shots from each run that displayed the presence of a GMan or Bootlegger to train the model and provide a proof of concept.

Object Detection

By using and image labeling software, I was able to compile the labels and their locations to train the YOLOv5 model to detect the GMan and Bootlegger.
