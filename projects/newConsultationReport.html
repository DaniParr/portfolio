Consultation Report Generator

Description

June 2022

At Beck College Prep, students rely on their score reports to guage their progress in studying for the ACT and SATs. Therefore, it is vital that score reports get to students as quick as possible. By utilizing the Monday.com API and Node.js I was able to design and implement a Consultation Report UI for our program coordinator and supervisor to use. It allowed for instant emailing from website to the client, saving 1 hour for every student's score report that was manually made.

Features

Dynamic Sizing

Using ReactJS allowed for dynamic sizing of all the information placed in the template. All items could be offset to the needs of the designer making formatting seemless.

Auto-Fill

Using MongoDB to store all student report information over the years, I implemented an auto-fill feature that would take information from MongoDB and place it in its respective field.

Version Control

Once the designer was happy with how the information was formatted on the template, they could save the draft as a PDF. The save would be listed and all the input information would be recorded to be finished later.
