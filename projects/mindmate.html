Robotech 2023: Mind Mate

March 2023

The theme of the Robotech 2023 was to create a product that incorporated AI involved education and mental health awareness. With Mind Mate, our team aimed to develop a safe community for people that can't find an outlet to speak about their mental health to find one. We would provide users with a survey to guage their mental state and match them with other user's online that shared similar outlooks. We passed the results from the survey to the AI to tailor conversation and give advice. My role in the project was orchestrating the development from the initial idea to the implementation.

https://devpost.com/software/mindful-messenger

Features

GPT 3

We utilized GPT3 AI to prompt the AI that maintained conversation with the user to be supportive and give friendly advice.

Firebase Realtime DB

We utilized Firebase Realtime DB to host messaging between users.

Anonymus Authentication

In order to make conversations anonymous and save conversations between users, we used Firebase's anonymous authentication to keep user identity out of the conversation.
