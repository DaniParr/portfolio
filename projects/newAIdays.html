AI Days: VChat - AI Generated UX

Description

🔗 Devpost

October 2023

In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, customer-centricity and ease of access are paramount. Our project, vchat, is a pioneering solution designed to revolutionize the way users interact with and access Verizon's extensive product offerings. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, this tool empowers users to effortlessly find and engage with Verizon products through tailored, intuitive user interfaces, which adapt dynamically to user prompts and preferences.

Features

Adaptive UI Generation

The core functionality of our AI tool revolves around its ability to generate user interfaces that dynamically adjust based on user interactions and queries. Whether you're searching for a new smartphone, a wireless plan, or home security solutions, the interface evolves to provide you with the most relevant information and options.

Generative Nav Bar

We have a nav bar that populates each query with respective links to resources using prompt engineering, retreival augmented generation and context augmented generation.

Product Recommendations

The tool doesn't just stop at providing information; it also offers personalized product recommendations based on your preferences and previous interactions. This ensures that users can easily discover products that align with their needs and desires.

As Backend Developer...

As the Backend Developer for a Generative UI project, I meticulously crafted prompts to align with the desired output format for GPT-3.5 responses. I established endpoints, employed Postman for thorough testing, and implemented post-processing functions to handle and refine GPT-3.5 outputs, ensuring precision in our project's generative responses.
